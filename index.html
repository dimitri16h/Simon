Welcome

Welcome to Simon! This is a fun color-based game of memory. The instructions are simple:

  • The game will show a signal.
  • You must repeat the signal by pressing the color shown.
  • You may also use the arrow buttons, if you wish.
  • The game will show increasingly more complicated patterns.
  • See how long you can keep up!

    • When you are ready, press the Play Button below. Enjoy!
